To build the project in Glitch (https://glitch.com/) and visualize from an ARCore mobile device
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1- Login and create a New Project | hello-webpage 
2- Replace index.html and style.css files code by the ones here.
3- Upload the three .glb models in the assets folder.
4- Click on each uploaded model to copy their URLs.
5- Replace the href URLs of the models inside the index.html with the ones of your own models.
6- Click on Share | Live app to copy the URL to your Glitch app
7- Copy it in your ARCore supporting device and when the page loads, click on the little box on the bottom right-hand and then on "View in your space" to see each model in AR.
